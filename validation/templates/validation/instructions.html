In this HIT, you are matched with another worker who is also participating in this HIT. You will interact with this same worker for the entire task. All interactions take place in real time.

Please be considerate of the other worker's time!

In this task, there are multiple rounds. In each round, you face a simple decision: You can either Cooperate or Defect. Whether you choose to cooperate or defect affects both your payoffs and those of the worker you've been paired with. You will only be able to see the other worker's decision after you have submitted yours.

Furthermore, each round can be either High or Low stakes. In the high stakes version, the bonus payoffs are considerably higher (see below for exact numbers). 1 point is worth $0.01.

In the first round, you will start off in the {{Constants.default_stakes}} stakes game. In each subsequent round, you will play the high stakes game if you both chose to cooperate in the previous round. Otherwise you will play the low stakes version. In other words, if one of you chose to defect in the previous round, you will play the low stakes version in the next round.

There will be at least 20 rounds of the paired task. After the 20th round, there will be a 50% chance that another round will follow. After that round, there will again be a 50% chance that another round will follow, and so on, until the paired task ends. When the paired task is over, you will see a brief questionnaire before you can submit the HIT.
