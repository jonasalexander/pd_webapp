In this study, you will be randomly and anonymously paired with another participant. Each of you simultaneously and privately chooses whether you want to cooperate or defect. In addition to determining your payoffs as outlined below, if one of you defects, you will also be forced to play a low-stakes game in the following rounds. You can only move back to the high-stakes game after a round in which you both choose to cooperate. You will play at least {{Constants.base_rounds}} rounds and after that will continue playing each additional round with 50% probability. You will be paired with the same other participants for all rounds.
